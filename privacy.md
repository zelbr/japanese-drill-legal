## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in connection with the App.

and is not implied by mere use of the App.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2-1. Information You Provide

Japanese Drill does not require account creation and does not collect your name, email address, or any other personally identifiable information directly.

### 2-2. Automatically Collected Information

The App uses **Google AdMob** (provided by Google LLC) to display advertisements. AdMob may automatically collect the following information:

- **Advertising ID** — A resettable device identifier used for interest-based advertising (Google Advertising ID on Android; IDFA on iOS)
- **Device information** — Device model, operating system version, language settings
- **IP address** — Used to infer approximate geographic location for ad targeting
- **App usage data** — Ad impressions, clicks, and interaction events
- **Diagnostic data** — Crash logs and performance data

This data is collected and processed by Google in accordance with [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

### 2-3. Locally Stored Data

All learning progress (XP, streak, spaced repetition card data) is stored **locally on your device only** using Hive, a local database. This data is never transmitted to our servers or any third party.

---

## 3. How We Use Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. Information collected via AdMob is used solely for the following purposes:

- Displaying relevant advertisements within the App
- Measuring ad performance and detecting invalid traffic
- Complying with legal obligations

We also use **Firebase Analytics** (Google LLC) to collect aggregate, anonymized usage data such as app open events and screen views. This data does not identify individual users and is processed in accordance with [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

---

## 4. Advertising

### 4-1. Google AdMob

The App displays banner ads and optional rewarded video ads via Google AdMob. Rewarded ads are entirely voluntary — you may choose to watch a video ad to earn an in-app XP bonus, but you are never required to do so.

AdMob may use your Advertising ID to show personalized ads based on your interests. You can opt out of personalized advertising at any time:

- **Android:** Settings → Google → Ads → Delete advertising ID (Android 12+) or opt out of Ads Personalization
- **iOS:** Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking → disable "Allow Apps to Request to Track"

### 4-2. EU/EEA and UK Users — Consent

If you are located in the European Union, European Economic Area, or United Kingdom, we use Google's User Messaging Platform (UMP) to request your consent before collecting any data for personalized advertising, in compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the UK GDPR.

You may withdraw or change your consent at any time within the App's settings.

- **Contact form data**: Responses submitted via our Google Forms contact form are stored by Google and used solely for the purpose of responding to your inquiry.
